BioNLP Shared Task 2013
Genia Event Extraction (GE) Task
"A shared task of literature mining for knowledge of NFκB pathway."
Overview Details Dev. Evaluation
Currently, IE evaluation is available in the same form as BioNLP-ST 2011.
Additional evaluation (KB evaluation) will become available shortly.

Development set evaluation

  • This service is available at any time for the convenience of system development.
    • Be careful not to overfit your model to the development data set.

Step 1. Provide your registered e-mail address

E-mail address:

  • To have your e-mail address registered, subscribe to the bionlp-st list.
    • E-mail to majordomo (at) bionlp-st (dot) org with "subscribe bionlp-st" in the body of the message.

Step 2. Upload the tarball file including your predicted .a2 files

File to Upload:

  • Tar and gzip all your predicted .a2 files, and submit the tarball file, e.g., filename.tar.gz.

Step 3. Submit and wait for the evaluation results

  • Evaluation may take time. Please wait for a while after submission.
